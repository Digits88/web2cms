{{=T('Welcome to web2cms!')}}

web2cms is a web2py-based Content Management System.

At the moment it is under development, and this is just an experimental version. Future usable versions will have something different in place of this introduction page. Now I'm just writing some text just in order to fill this gap and avoid leaving a blank page around.

Project status

The current project status is pre-alpha, pretty much nothing is usable yet, although the whole structure is being defined.
Feel free to try it out but keep in mind that very few things actually work (content creation and display is ok, updating it is work-in-progress; the whole extension modules part is completely under development; ...).
